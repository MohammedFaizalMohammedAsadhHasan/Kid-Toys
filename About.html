Our Story

Learn about our passion for quality toys and child development

About Kids Toys

Welcome to KiDS TOYS, where imagination comes to life! Founded in 2010, we've been dedicated to providing high-quality, safe, and educational toys that inspire creativity and learning in children of all ages.

Our journey began when our founder, Sarah Johnson, couldn't find toys that were both fun and educational for her own children. Frustrated with the lack of options, she decided to create a toy store that would focus on products that help children develop important skills while having fun.

Our Mission

At KiDS TOYS, we believe that play is the most important work of childhood. Our mission is to:

  • Provide toys that stimulate imagination and creativity
  • Offer products that support cognitive and physical development
  • Ensure all our toys meet the highest safety standards
  • Promote sustainable and eco-friendly toy options
  • Make playtime both fun and educational

Our Team

We're a team of parents, educators, and toy enthusiasts who carefully select every product in our store. Each toy is tested by children (and sometimes by our team members who are kids at heart!) to ensure it meets our high standards for quality, durability, and fun.

Quality Promise

Every toy in our store is:

  • Made from non-toxic, child-safe materials
  • Tested for durability and safety
  • Selected for its educational or developmental value
  • Backed by our 100% satisfaction guarantee
